POV

A practitioner's point of view on identity, access governance, and what comes next.

Thoughts from 8+ years of owning IAM product strategy — on the problems that don't show up in vendor documentation and the questions that don't have clean answers yet.

Jun 2026
NHI · Governance
We're governing the credential. We should be governing the behavior.

Non-human identities don't join, move, or leave. They get created for a purpose, become critical, and outlive everyone's expectations. The real problem isn't credential rotation — it's that nobody owns what the identity actually does.

Access Governance · IGA
Access reviews are designed for compliance. They're not designed for reviewers.

On paper, the process is clean. In practice, reviewers face long lists of entitlements they don't fully understand — and when context is missing, approval is the path of least resistance. That's a product problem, not a people problem.

Jun 2026
AI Agents · Identity
AI agents aren't apps. IAM needs a third identity class.

Most teams are governing AI agents like service accounts. But an agent can take action based on context — across tools, data, and workflows. The IAM question changes from "what access does this account have?" to "who gave the agent authority, and what should require approval?"
